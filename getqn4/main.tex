Let 
\begin{align}
	\vec{A}+\vec{B}+\vec{C} &= \myvec{8\\1\\-12}  \\
	\abs{\myvec{a\\b\\c}} &= \sqrt{a^2 + b^2 + c^2} \\
	\implies	\abs{\vec{a}+\vec{b}+\vec{c}} &= \sqrt{209}  \\
\end{align}
Hence, the answer to the given question is $\sqrt{209}$.
